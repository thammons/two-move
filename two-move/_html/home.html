Welcome to Two Move!

In this game, you have two moves:

  • Move: advance forward once squre in the direction of the indiciator.
    • Press the up or down arrow keys, or 'W' or 'S'
  • Turn: turn right. Stay in the square you are in, but pivot right.
    • Press the left or right arrow keys, or 'A' or 'D'

...but you also have a few tools:

  • Teleport: If you get stuck on an unsolveable map.
    • Press 'T'
  • Flashlight: see the squares around you.
    • Press 'F'

Good luck solving the puzzles!
